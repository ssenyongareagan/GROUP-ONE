Our Awards

Merit Scholarship

This scholarship is awarded to students with exceptional academic performance. Criteria include high GPA, rigorous coursework, and outstanding letters of recommendation.

Academic Achievement Scholarship

Recognizes students who have demonstrated significant improvement and commitment to their academic pursuits. Ideal for students who have overcome challenges to achieve their academic goals.

Research Scholarship

Supports students involved in groundbreaking research. Applicants must submit a research proposal, letters of recommendation, and proof of academic achievement in their field of study.

Leadership Scholarship

Awarded to students who have demonstrated exceptional leadership skills through community service, student government, or other leadership roles. Requires a personal statement and letters of recommendation.
