Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I create a scholarship?

To create a scholarship, you need to sign in to your administrator account, go to the 'Create Scholarship' section, fill in the necessary details such as scholarship name, criteria, and deadline, and then submit it for approval.

2. How do I submit an application?

To submit an application, log in to your student account, browse available scholarships, select the one you wish to apply for, fill in the required details, and upload any necessary documents before submitting your application.

3. How do I evaluate applications as a reviewer?

Reviewers can log in to their accounts, navigate to the 'Review Applications' section, select the applications assigned to them, review the provided information and documents, and provide their scores and feedback.

4. How do I award scholarships as an administrator?

As an administrator, you can log in to your account, go to the 'Award Scholarships' section, review the applications and scores provided by reviewers, select the recipients, and officially award the scholarships.

Troubleshooting

Login issues

If you are experiencing login issues, make sure you are using the correct username and password. If you have forgotten your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link to reset it. For further assistance, contact our support team.

Application submission errors

If you encounter errors while submitting your application, ensure that all required fields are filled out correctly and that your documents are in the supported formats. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.

Review and feedback issues

If you are having issues with reviewing applications or providing feedback, make sure you are logged in to your reviewer account and that you have access to the assigned applications. For further assistance, contact our support team.
